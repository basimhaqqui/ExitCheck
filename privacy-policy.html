ExitCheck Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

Summary: ExitCheck is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Introduction

ExitCheck ("the App") is a location-based checklist reminder application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Data We Collect

ExitCheck collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Home Location: The geographic coordinates you set as your home, used to create a geofence for exit detection.
  • Checklist Items: The items you add to your personal checklist.
  • Usage Statistics: Your streak count, exit history, and forgotten item counts.
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences within the App.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data is:

  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never used for advertising or analytics
  • Protected by your device's built-in security

Location Data

ExitCheck requires "Always" location permission to detect when you leave your home, even when the App is closed. This location data is:

  • Used solely for geofencing (detecting exit from your home zone)
  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never sent to any server or third party
  • Not tracked or logged beyond the immediate geofence check

Third-Party Services

ExitCheck does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain any SDKs that collect user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data with anyone. Period.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete the App
  • Clear the App's data through iOS Settings
  • Manually delete items within the App

Children's Privacy

ExitCheck does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to function.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:

  • View all your data within the App
  • Delete any checklist items at any time
  • Reset your home location
  • Delete all data by removing the App

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
