Frequently Asked Questions

Why does ExitCheck need "Always" location access?

ExitCheck uses geofencing to detect when you leave your home zone. For this to work even when the app is closed, iOS requires "Always" location permission. Don't worry - we only monitor your home zone and your location data never leaves your device.

Will ExitCheck drain my battery?

No. ExitCheck uses Apple's efficient geofencing technology, which is built into iOS. Unlike GPS tracking apps, geofencing uses minimal battery because it only activates when you cross your home boundary.

How do I change my home location?

Go to Settings within the app and tap on "Home Location" to update your home address or adjust the zone radius.

Can I have multiple home locations?

Currently, ExitCheck supports one home location. We're considering adding support for multiple locations (like work or gym) in future updates.

Why didn't I get a notification when I left home?

Check the following:
1. Ensure location permission is set to "Always" in iOS Settings
2. Make sure notifications are enabled for ExitCheck
3. Verify your home location is set correctly
4. Check that the app shows "Active" status on the home screen

Is my data private?

Yes, completely. All your data (location, checklist items, statistics) is stored locally on your device only. We never collect, transmit, or share any of your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored on your device, you can delete everything by uninstalling the app. You can also delete individual checklist items by swiping left on them.
